CML CASE SIMULATION

CASE SCENARIO 1

This case scenario describes a 48 years old patient who was diagnosed with chronic phase CML based on the histopathologic findings in the peripheral blood and the Philadelphia (Ph) chromosome in bone marrow cells and is treated with Nilotinib 300 mg BD. The presentation also details evidence of efficacy of Nilotinib as a first line agent based on ENESTnd trial (6 year trial). The results of the ENEST1st trial and their application in daily practice are also discussed.

Duration : 3.00 Min
