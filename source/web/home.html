Introduction to CML

Chronic myelogenous leukaemia (CML) will affect over 100,000 patients worldwide every year and represent a significant global health burden. The introduction of tyrosine kinase inhibitors (TKIs) has increased the life expectancy of patients with CML and also an increased prevalence of the condition. It has also been observed that the prevalence of survivors with CML has increased by 4000 to 5000 per year since the approval of oral TKI therapy in 2001 and is expected to plateau at 181,000 by 2050.

About CML Interact

CML Interact is a unified platform which brings together the best minds in the management of CML from around the world while also bringing together several resources that can be useful for better patient outcomes into one portal.


There is a wealth of information about CML available from several prestigious groups and organizations. However, all of this information is present in various websites placing demands on time to access it. Despite the availability of all this information some experts believe that the results of scientific advances may not be translated in to public health benefits. CML Interact is an endeavour that attempts to address this by bringing together resources of information and experts from various geographies together to facilitate better understanding of the information and assimilation into clinical practice.

1. Unable to register

  • It is mandatory to agree to the terms of use and provide consent. Kindly check the box after going through the details and before clicking/tapping on submit icon.

2. Do not have Access code. How to register?

  • Approach the Novartis representative
  • Alternatively please write to us from the ‘Contact us’ section

3. Successfully registered but unable to Login

  • Kindly check your registered email ID for an account activation email from 'info@cmlinteract.com'
  • Post activation of your account you will be able to Login with the registered credentials.

4. Forgot password/Reset password

  • On the landing page click on ‘Forgot Password’, enter the registered email ID and submit. User will receive an automated email on their registered email ID, with a link to reset password. User can then login using the new password

5. Accessibility on Mobile/Tablet

  • You can download CML Interact app from windows; android and apple store.
  • Once registered on either laptop/desktop or mobile app, you can directly login on any device.
